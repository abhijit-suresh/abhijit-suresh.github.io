More About Me

I am a 5th year PhD student in Computer science, Neuroscience and Cognitive science at the University of Colorado,Boulder. My research interests are applied machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing and cognitive neuroscience.

Hello!

At present, I'm working on the cross-roads of computer science, cogntive science and neuroscience.

What does creating lesions in DNNs tell us about the existence of fuctionally specific units selective to broad image categories?. I propose a novel approach to lesion individual units from deep neural networks with millions of units. Results show that lesioning can create functionally specfic deficits in top-5 accuracy for animate and inanimate categories.

As a K-12 math teacher, how do you use effective discourse strategies to ensure student engagement and engage in rich classroom discussions?. I'm using state-of-the-art transformers to provide teachers with implicit feedback on their classroom discourse with performance upto 80%.
